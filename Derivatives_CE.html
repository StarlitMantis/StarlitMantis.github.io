A word of warning. Being able to calculate derivatives with you calculator is a massive time saver but you should make sure to not use it as a crutch and not learn/understand the content fully. Calculus 1 is a very difficult class and once you get a little behind it is much easier to fall much farther behind. Curve this by keeping up for the content and knowing when to ask your peers, professor and/or tutors for help.
